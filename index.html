My Story

I have nearly 10 years’ experience working as a teacher, facilitator, coach, consultant and mentor, and at the heart of what I do is to help people & organisations reach their full potential. I work with those who are aspiring to make changes, whether that relates to achieving specific goals, overcoming challenges, shifting mindset, or transforming the way they live, work and operate. At the heart of what I do is to help people create meaning and impact in their lives and work. Often, this means guiding them to evaluate their definition of “success” and what it looks like for them, and then, through powerful conversations and insight, helping them to move towards this.
