Coaching within Organisations

As a Professional Development coach, I offer bespoke coaching packages to businesses based on working one-to-one with individuals over an extended period on the issues they are facing. The agenda of the coaching programme can either be set by the individual themselves, by the organisation/business, or based on the results of 360 reviews, which I offer as part of the coaching package.

I have experience coaching on a wide variety of topics, from communication to decision-making, from management styles to self-belief, and from client relationships to motivation. The impact this has had on my clients mirrors that of the research – coaching clients see gains in productivity, drive, and engagement with their work, and reduced stress and burnout.

If you are committed to helping your employees grow, perform better and be happier and more engaged with the work they are doing, get in touch for a free call/visit to your offices so we can discuss your needs.

Bespoke Packages

Each organisation/business has unique requirements including number & job positions of employees to be coached, duration of the coaching programme, and the specific challenges to be addressed. As such, I don't offer prescribed coaching packages, but instead will design them according to your needs.

Potential features of the coaching package include:​

  • 360 reviews
  • Line manager meetings
  • Goal-setting/review meetings
  • Regular coaching sessions (usually 1-2 per month)
  • Progress updates & final report
  • Psychometric test integration
  • Ongoing email support

If you want to discuss how I could create a coaching package based on your organisation's requirements, get in touch and we can schedule a call or visit.
